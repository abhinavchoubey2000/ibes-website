Benefits of Management System Documentation & Certifications

The ISO series of standards have been developed to enable organizations to have process-based management systems. This should enable them to understand their Legal/customers’ requirements and improve Safety, Business Performance & customer satisfaction, through the review and improvement of their internal activities. The benefits of using the standards include:

  • The connection of management systems to organizational processes.
  • The encouragement of a natural progression towards improved organizational performance, via
  • Use of the Management Principles.
  • Adoption of a ‘process approach’.
  • Emphasis of the role of top management.
  • Requirements for the establishment of measurable objectives at relevant functions and levels.
  • Being orientated toward ‘continual improvement’ and ‘customer satisfaction’, including the monitoring of information on ‘customer satisfaction’ as a measure of system performance.
  • Measurement of the management system, processes and product. Consideration of statutory and regulatory requirements
  • Attention to resource availability.
  • Risk Management.
